<b>Description:</b>
<p>The 2018 edition of the Workshop on Web Archiving and Digital Libraries (WADL) will explore the 
integration of Web archiving and digital libraries. The workshop aims at addressing aspects covering 
the entire life cycle of digital resources and will also explore areas such as community building and 
ethical questions around Web archiving.</p>
In addition, the chairs will initiate the workshop proceedings being published in a special issue of 
IEEE TCDL Bulletin.

<p>WADL 2018 will cover all topics of interest, including but not limited to:</p>

<p>
<b>Objectives: </b></span></p><ul class="c10 lst-kix_list_7-0 start">
<li>to continue to build the community of people integrating
  Web archiving &amp; digital libraries 
<li>to help attendees learn about useful methods, systems,
  and software in this area
<li>to help chart future research and improved practice in this area
<li>to promote synergistic efforts including collaborative projects
 and proposals
<li>to produce an archival publication that will help advance technology
 and practice
</ul>
