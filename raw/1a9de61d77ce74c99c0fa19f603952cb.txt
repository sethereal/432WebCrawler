      <div class="main-content">
        <p>The 42nd International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development
in Information Retrieval will take place on July 21-25, 2019 in Paris.
The conference is backed up by the French Association for Information
Retrieval and Applications (<a href="http://www.asso-aria.org/" title="ARIA">ARIA</a>) which organizes the yearly IR French
CORIA conference.</p>
<p><a href="http://sigir.org" title="ACM SIGIR">SIGIR</a> is the premier international forum for the presentation of new
research results and for the demonstration of new systems and techniques
in information retrieval. The conference consists of five days of full
papers, short papers, demonstrations, tutorials and <a href="program/workshops" title="SIGIR 2019 Workshops">workshops</a> focused on
research and development in the area of information retrieval, as well
as an industry track and social events.</p>
<p>SIGIR 2019 is now over; thanks to all participants, and see you in <!-- raw HTML omitted -->Xi&rsquo;An<!-- raw HTML omitted -->.</p>
